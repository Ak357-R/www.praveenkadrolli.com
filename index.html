Frequently Asked Questions

Common Investor Questions

Get answers to some of the most common questions about mutual funds, SIPs, financial planning and investments.

What is a SIP and how does it work?
A Systematic Investment Plan (SIP) allows you to invest a fixed amount regularly in mutual funds, helping build wealth through disciplined investing and compounding.
How much should I invest every month?
The ideal amount depends on your financial goals, income, risk appetite and investment horizon.
Can NRIs invest in mutual funds in India?
Yes. NRIs can invest in Indian mutual funds subject to regulatory requirements and KYC compliance.
How often do you review portfolios?
We regularly review portfolios and provide updates to ensure investments remain aligned with goals.
Do you provide insurance guidance?
Yes. We provide guidance for Health Insurance, Term Insurance and Vehicle Insurance solutions.
