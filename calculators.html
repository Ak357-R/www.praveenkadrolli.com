Frequently Asked Questions

Common Investor Questions

What is the ideal SIP amount?

There is no fixed ideal amount. Start with what you can comfortably invest consistently and increase gradually through step-up SIPs.

How accurate are these calculators?

These calculators use standard financial formulas widely used across the investment industry. Actual returns may differ based on market performance.

Can I modify my SIP later?

Yes. SIP amounts can generally be increased, decreased or paused depending on fund house policies.

Are mutual fund returns guaranteed?

No. Mutual Fund investments are subject to market risks and returns are not guaranteed.
