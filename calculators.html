FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is SIP?

A Systematic Investment Plan (SIP) allows you to invest a fixed amount regularly into mutual funds and benefit from rupee cost averaging and compounding.

What return rate should I use?

For long-term equity mutual funds, many investors use 10%–15% assumptions for planning purposes. Actual returns may vary.

Are SIP returns guaranteed?

No. Mutual Fund investments are subject to market risks and returns are not guaranteed.

Can I increase my SIP later?

Yes. You can increase your SIP amount anytime through a step-up SIP strategy.
